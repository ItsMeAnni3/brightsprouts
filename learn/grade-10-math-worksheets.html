What your child will learn — Geometry: Triangles, Circles & Proofs

  • Triangle angles always sum to 180°. Know two angles? The third is free!
  • Pythagorean Theorem: in a right triangle, a² + b² = c². Legs 3 and 4 give hypotenuse 5, since 9 + 16 = 25.
  • Circle formulas: circumference C = 2πr, area A = πr². (π ≈ 3.14159 — the same for EVERY circle in the universe.)
  • Area toolbox: rectangle = l·w, triangle = ½·b·h, and volume of a box = l·w·h.

Sample worksheet questions

1. Two angles of a triangle are 40° and 51°. Find the third angle. ____
Answer: 89°
2. A right triangle has legs 3 and 4. Find the hypotenuse. ____
Answer: 5
3. Find the area of a circle with radius 2. (Use π ≈ 3.14) ____
Answer: ≈ 12.56
4. Find the volume of a box 9 × 11 × 5. ____
Answer: 495
5. Two angles of a triangle are 71° and 67°. Find the third angle. ____
Answer: 42°
