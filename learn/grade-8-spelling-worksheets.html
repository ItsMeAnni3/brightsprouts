What your child will learn — The Heavyweights: Champion-Level Words

  • 'Accommodate' is generous — it accommodates two C's AND two M's.
  • 'Colonel' sounds like 'kernel' — English borrowed it from French and kept the weird spelling.
  • Break monsters into rooms: ar-chi-tec-ture, cor-re-spond.
  • Spell it backwards once — it forces your brain to see every letter.

Sample worksheet questions

1. Unscramble the word: "daoaarsbms" (it starts with "a")
Answer: ambassador
2. Fill in the missing letters: a n _ i e _ y
Answer: anxiety
3. Circle the correct spelling: characteristic chaaracteristic charaacteristic
Answer: characteristic
4. 🗣️ Spelling test: a grown-up reads answer #4 from the answer key out loud — you write it!
Answer: correspond
5. Unscramble the word: "eveidce" (it starts with "d")
Answer: deceive
