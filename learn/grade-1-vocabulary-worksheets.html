    Sample worksheet questions

    1. Unscramble this word: "vaber" (hint: not afraid to try)
    Answer: brave
    2. Fill in the blank: I ________ my crayons with my friend.
    Answer: share
    3. Unscramble this word: "tgain" (hint: very, very big)
    Answer: giant
    4. Write your own sentence using the word "curious" (meaning: wanting to know more)
    Answer: Sentences will vary — check the word is used correctly.
    5. Fill in the blank: Be ________, the baby is sleeping.
    Answer: quiet

    These are generated fresh every time — press New Worksheet in the app for a brand-new set, with a printable answer key.
