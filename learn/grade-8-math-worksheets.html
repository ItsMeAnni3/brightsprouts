What your child will learn — Linear Equations & Functions

  • A function is a rule: each input gets exactly one output. Think vending machine — press B4, get one snack.
  • y = mx + b is a line: m is the slope (steepness), b is the y-intercept (starting point).
  • Slope = rise/run = change in y ÷ change in x. Between (1,3) and (3,7): slope = (7−3)/(3−1) = 2.
  • Real life: cost = 0.10 × texts + 20 is a line! $20 flat fee (b), 10 cents per text (m).

Sample worksheet questions

Answer: 14

Answer: x = 7
