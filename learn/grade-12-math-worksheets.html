What your child will learn — Pre-Calculus & Statistics for Life

  • Logarithms undo exponents: log₂(8) = 3 because 2³ = 8. They measure earthquakes (Richter) and sound (decibels)!
  • Sequences: arithmetic adds a constant (3, 7, 11, ...); geometric multiplies (2, 6, 18, ...). Sum formulas predict totals fast.
  • Statistics: mean (average), median (middle — resists outliers!), mode (most common), standard deviation (spread). One billionaire in a room skyrockets the MEAN income but barely moves the MEDIAN.
  • Probability: independent events multiply. P(two heads) = ½ × ½ = ¼. Compound interest A = P(1 + r/n)ⁿᵗ is 'the eighth wonder of the world.'
